Installer

Flash the firmware

three steps · no toolchain · right in the browser

  1. Plug in
    Use desktop Chrome or Edge and connect the Bisc8 to your computer over USB-C.
  2. Click Flash
    Hit Flash Bisc8, pick the serial port in the dialog, and let it erase and install. It takes about a minute.
  3. Configure
    When it reboots, join the Bisc8 Wi-Fi network and set up your network, language, and oracle in the captive portal.

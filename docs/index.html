Installer

Flash the firmware

three steps · no toolchain · right in the browser

  1. Plug in
    Open this page in desktop Chrome or Edge and plug the biscuit in over USB-C.
  2. Click Flash
    Hit Flash Bisc8, pick the port, and let it write bootloader, table and app. About a minute — go butter some toast.
  3. Configure
    When it reboots, join the Bisc8-XXXX hotspot, open the portal, and hand it your Wi-Fi, language and OpenAI key.
